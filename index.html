Hi, I'm Paola

My first love is boxing, this discipline has helped me a lot in life, both in becoming more tenacious and not letting life's adversities discourage me, and in getting to know my body, learning to respect it and take care of it, it is thanks to boxing that my life has taken the trajectory it has today, and I became first a boxing coach and then a fitness trainer.
For years I had a rather complicated relationship with my body and my perception of it, but thanks to sport and study, I have not only learnt to love my body and feel comfortable in it.

My holistic approach

Many of us have a difficult relationship with our own body and health.
I want to accompany you through my workouts on a path where you will first learn to love and respect your body,
to value its uniqueness, a path that will teach you to feel light, free,
and happy to live in the wonderful home that is your body.

Increase your Self-Confidence

I will help you feel good about your body, increase your self-confidence, and achieve the goal you have set for yourself.
Training with me means embarking on a path where there are no promises of quick fixes or weight-loss tricks, but one step at a time you will reach each goal thanks to your tenacity above all and then to my guidance, I will help you with nutrition and time management to make your plan doable and reach your goals.
In difficult moments I will be at your side to support and motivate you.
You will learn about your body, how to nourish it with exercise and healthy food and healthy habits.

With me you will learn to stop procrastinating!
We often put off a big change because we think we don't have time, or that it is too difficult, or that it is too late to make it.
None of these assumptions are true, if you have a busy life, full time job, you are a parent, you work extra hours, don’t worry!
We will find a solution together that is tailored to you;
it is better to exercise three times a week for 25 minutes than not to move at all.
When we start something for the first time we are always intimidated and it always seems bigger than it really is, you will learn to do the exercises in progression, I will adapt the workouts to the needs of your body and the responses it gives during them, you will make progress at the right time.

I am too old!

It's too late, I am too old!
The most important thing in life is to value yourself, to love yourself and take care of yourself;
